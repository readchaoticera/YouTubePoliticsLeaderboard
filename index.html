About this leaderboard

  • This board tracks leading political YouTube channels — including news outlets, commentary shows, podcasts, advocacy organizations, and independent creators whose primary focus is U.S. politics and current affairs.
  • Total Subscribers is the channel's total subscriber count. Q2 Sub Growth is net subscribers gained (or lost) during Q2, and Q2 Views is video views during Q2, and includes views from YouTube Shorts. Channels without a reported Q2 figure are shown as 0; negative values reflect losses or reported corrections.
  • The charts are color-coded by lean: the bubble chart plots every channel sized by total subscribers, and the two row charts rank the top 50 by Q2 subscriber growth and Q2 views (about 25 visible; scroll within a chart for the rest). Hover any mark for detail; click to open the channel.
  • Lean is a subjective editorial judgment by Chaotic Era, not a scientific measure — reasonable people will disagree. We use four buckets: Left, Left Adjacent, Right Adjacent, and Right — where the "Adjacent" buckets capture a softer, center-of-spectrum tilt. Channels we haven't assessed (or that don't fit cleanly) show as Unrated. Spot one you'd label differently? Let us know.
  • URL links to each channel on YouTube. Where we have a verified channel handle it links directly; otherwise it opens a YouTube search that resolves to the channel.
  • Click any column header to re-sort. Use the search box to filter by channel or lean. Data is refreshed periodically.
