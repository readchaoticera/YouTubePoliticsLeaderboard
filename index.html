About this leaderboard

  • This board tracks leading political YouTube channels — including news outlets, commentary shows, podcasts, advocacy organizations, and independent creators whose primary focus is U.S. politics and current affairs.
  • Subscribers is the channel's total subscriber count. Total Views is all-time cumulative video views.
  • 90-Day Growth and 90-Day Views measure subscribers and views added over the trailing 90 days. A few channels don't report a trailing figure and show N/A; negative values reflect reported corrections.
  • Lean is a subjective editorial judgment by Chaotic Era, not a scientific measure — reasonable people will disagree. We use two buckets: Left (left-leaning, progressive, or Democrat) and Right (right-leaning, conservative, or Republican). Channels we haven't assessed (or that don't fit cleanly) show as Unrated. Spot one you'd label differently? Let us know.
  • URL links to each channel on YouTube. Where we have a verified channel handle it links directly; otherwise it opens a YouTube search that resolves to the channel.
  • Click any column header to re-sort. Use the search box to filter by channel or lean. Data is refreshed periodically.
